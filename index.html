Phenom 300 Memory Items

Memory Items

Instructions

Dual Engine Failure

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Crew Oxygen Masks DON, 100%
  3. Communication ESTABLISH

Emergency Descent

  1. SIGNS/OUTLET Switch PED-BELTS/OFF
  2. Altitude Selector 10,000ft or MEA
  3. Thrust Levers IDLE
  4. SPEED BRAKE Switch OPEN
  5. Airspeed 250/MMO MAXIMUM
  6. LDG GEAR Lever DN
  7. Transponder 7700
  8. ATC NOTIFY

Emergency Evacuation

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Emergency/Parking Brake ON
  3. Start/Stop Knobs STOP
  4. SHUTOFF 1&2 Buttons PUSH IN
  5. PRESN MODE Switch MAN
  6. Pressurization DUMP Button PUSH IN
  7. ATC NOTIFY
  8. Emergency Evacuation PERFORM
  9. BATT 1 & 2 Switches OFF

Engine Abnormal Start

  1. Start/Stop Knob STOP

Engine Fire, Severe Damage or Separation

  1. Thrust Lever IDLE
  2. Start/Stop Knob STOP
  3. Fire Shutoff Button PUSH IN

Rejected Takeoff (at or below V1)

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Brakes APPLY MAXIMUM
  3. Directional Control MAINTAIN

Engine Failure Above V1

    At VR rotate to:

  1. FLAP POSITION 1: 10.5°
  2. FLAP POSITION 2: 8°

    3. With positive rate of climb:

  3. LDG GEAR Lever UP
  4. Airspeed V2

Inadvertent Pusher Actuation

  1. QUICK DISCONNECT Button PRESS
  2. PUSHER CUTOUT Button PUSH IN

Landing Gear Cannot Be Moved Up

  1. DN LCK REL Button PRESS
  2. LDG GEAR Lever UP

Cabin Altitude High

  1. Crew Oxygen Masks DON, 100%
  2. Communication ESTABLISH
  3. SIGNS/OUTLET Switch PED-BELTS/OFF
  4. Altitude Selector 10,000ft or MEA
  5. Thrust Levers IDLE
  6. SPEED BRAKE Switch OPEN
  7. Airspeed 250/MMO MAXIMUM
  8. LDG GEAR Lever DN
  9. Transponder 7700
  10. ATC NOTIFY

Electrical Emergency

  1. PRESN MODE Switch MAN
  2. CABIN ALT Switch HOLD DN FOR 10 SECONDS

Electrical Emergency Transfer Fail

  1. ELEC EMER Button PUSH IN

Smoke/Fire/Fumes

  1. Crew Oxygen Masks DON, EMER then 100%
  2. Oxygen Mask Dilution Valve CLOSED
  3. Smoke Goggles DON
  4. Communication ESTABLISH
  5. Pressurization DUMP Button PUSH IN

Smoke Evacuation

  1. Crew Oxygen Masks DON, EMER THEN 100%
  2. Oxygen Mask Dilution Valve CLOSED
  3. Smoke Goggles DON
  4. Communication ESTABLISH
  5. O2 SUPPLY CONTROL Knob CREW ONLY
  6. Pressurization DUMP Button PUSH IN
  7. ECS Knob OFF VENT

EGPWS Warning

  1. TO/GA Button PRESS
  2. Thrust MAX
  3. Pitch INCREASE TO AVOID TERRAIN
  4. Flaps 1
  5. Landing Gear UP
  6. Flaps UP

Windshear

  1. TO/GA Button PRESS
  2. Thrust MAX
  3. Pitch INCREASE TO ARREST DESCENT
  4. Configuration DO NOT CHANGE UNTIL SAFETY IS ASSURED
